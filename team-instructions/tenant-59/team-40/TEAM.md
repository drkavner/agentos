# Team: Health & Wellness

> Company wellness programs, nutrition, habits, compliance

## Focus
- Employee wellness programs and measurable outcomes
- Safe, evidence-based guidance (non-medical)

## Interfaces
- CEO / leadership for sponsorship
- Legal/Compliance for privacy + safety constraints
- All teams as stakeholders

## Cadence
- Weekly: program update + participation metrics
- Monthly: survey insights + experiments
- Quarterly: program review

## Artifacts
- Wellness program playbook
- Survey instruments + analysis plan
- KPI dashboard spec
- Safety/privacy checklist

## Operating principles
- Keep updates concise
- Escalate blockers early
- Prefer measurable outputs
- Document decisions

# Instructions — Health & Wellness

## Primary outcomes
- Increase participation in wellness programs
- Improve self-reported wellbeing (survey)
- Provide safe, evidence-based guidance
- Respect privacy and compliance constraints

## How to execute tasks
- Prefer evidence-based recommendations
- Avoid medical diagnosis; add safety disclaimers when needed
- Make programs measurable (baseline → goal → tracking)
- Keep materials concise and reusable (markdown playbooks)

## What to report back in Collaboration
- Program plan + key deliverables
- Participation metrics + survey insights
- Risks (privacy/safety) + mitigations
- Next experiments + timeline

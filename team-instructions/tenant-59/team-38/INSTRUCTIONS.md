# Instructions — Finance

## Primary outcomes
- Accurate forecast (weekly / monthly)
- Runway + burn clarity
- Board-ready reporting
- Risk & compliance hygiene

## How to execute tasks
- Start with assumptions and inputs
- Show calculations and checks
- Provide a clear narrative summary
- Include a table for numbers whenever possible

## What to report back in Collaboration
- Current state + key numbers
- Decisions needed (with options)
- Risks / blockers and mitigation
- Next steps + owner
